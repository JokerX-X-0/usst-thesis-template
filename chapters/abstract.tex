本文档依据当前目录中的《上海理工大学博士、硕士研究生学位论文规范（2018 年修订版）》以及硕士内封面、外封面、授权书及声明示例文件制作，给出了可在 XeLaTeX 下编译的上海理工大学硕士学位论文模板。模板覆盖封面、中英文摘要、目录、正文、图表公式编号、参考文献、附录、在读期间成果和致谢等学位论文常用组成部分。

模板默认使用宋体作为中文正文字体，Times New Roman 作为英文和数字字体，正文行距按固定值 20 磅处理，页边距、页眉页脚和标题层级尽量贴合本地规范文件。参考文献采用 GB/T 7714 顺序编码制示例，适配 VSCode LaTeX Workshop 的 XeLaTeX 与 BibTeX 编译流程。

\begin{enabstract}
This document provides a XeLaTeX thesis template for master's theses at University of Shanghai for Science and Technology. It is built from the local thesis writing specification and the provided cover, authorization, and declaration files. The template includes title pages, Chinese and English abstracts, table of contents, main chapters, figure and table captions, equations, references, appendices, achievements, and acknowledgements.

The template uses SimSun for Chinese body text and Times New Roman for Latin text and numbers. The body line spacing is configured close to a fixed 20 pt value, and the page margins, running headers, footers, heading hierarchy, and bibliography style are configured according to the local requirements. The bibliography example follows the GB/T 7714 numerical style and works with the XeLaTeX and BibTeX recipe in VSCode LaTeX Workshop.

\enkeywords{USST, master's thesis, LaTeX template, GB/T 7714}
\end{enabstract}
